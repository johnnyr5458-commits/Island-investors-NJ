## Option 2: List with a Real Estate Agent

Working with a licensed New Jersey real estate agent is the traditional approach. Your agent lists the home on the MLS, markets it to buyers, and negotiates on your behalf.

**Average time to close in South Jersey:** 60–90 days (from listing to settlement)

**Pros:** Potentially higher sale price  
**Cons:** Can take 3–6 months total, requires repairs and staging, 5–6% commission, deals can fall through

## Option 3: For Sale By Owner (FSBO)

You can list and sell the home yourself without an agent. This saves on commission but requires significant time and knowledge of NJ real estate law, contracts, and the local market.

**Best for:** Experienced sellers with time and real estate knowledge

## How to Choose the Right Option

Ask yourself these questions:

- **How fast do I need to close?** If you need cash in weeks, not months, a cash buyer is your best option.
- **Can I afford to wait?** If you have time, listing with an agent may net you more money.
- **Is the home in good condition?** Distressed properties rarely sell well on the open market.
- **Do I want to deal with showings and negotiations?** Cash sales eliminate all of that.

## What Affects How Fast You Sell?

In the South Jersey market, the following factors impact your time to sell:

- **Property condition** — Homes needing repairs take longer to sell traditionally
- **Pricing** — Overpriced homes sit on the market
- **Location** — Atlantic City and shore properties have seasonal demand
- **Time of year** — Spring and summer typically see faster sales in South Jersey
- **Financing contingencies** — Buyer financing delays are extremely common

## Common Questions About Selling Fast in South Jersey

**Do I need to make repairs before selling?**  
Not if you sell to a cash buyer. We buy homes in any condition — fire damage, water damage, code violations, deferred maintenance, and more.

**Will I get a fair price?**  
Cash buyers offer less than full retail market value because they take on the cost and risk of repairs and resale. However, when you factor in the money you save on repairs, agent fees, and carrying costs, the difference is often smaller than people expect.

**How do I know the offer is fair?**  
Ask for an explanation of how the offer was calculated. Reputable cash buyers — including Island Investors NJ — will show you comparable sales data and explain their offer transparently.
